Author: Dan Simmons
Description: A Hugo Award-winning science fiction novel, which details the journey of a special force of 'pilgrims' to the planet Hyperion, who were all connected to the planet in the past and are now tasked with unravelling its secrets. Story is comprised of multiple narrative accounts of the 'pilgrims' past connection to Hyperion, and is structured in the same way as the Canterbury Tales - stories told on-route to their destination!
