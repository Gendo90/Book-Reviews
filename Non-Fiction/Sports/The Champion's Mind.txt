Author: Jim Afremow, Ph.D
Description: An excellent book that will help an athlete or any other person undergoing some kind of training or performance situation - the book is written for athletes, but can improve the mentality of anyone! There is everything from positive thinking, how to deal with nerves and other game-day situations, post-game journaling and other methods to track progress and reinforce effort, Zen stories to provide wisdom, and even firsthand accounts from Olympians about how to think and train to become the best!
